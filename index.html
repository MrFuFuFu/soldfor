Sell

From “in stock” to “sold” in one tap

Open an item, tap Mark as Sold, type the price — SoldFor shows the profit you'll clear before you confirm, with platform fees and extra costs accounted for.

  • Live profit preview as you type
  • Sale platform, fees and GST recorded per sale
  • Status flips to Sold automatically
Profit

Know exactly what you made

Every sold item tells its money story: revenue, cost, profit and ROI up top, then a full history ledger — what you paid, what it sold for, and every fee along the way.

  • Profit and ROI at a glance
  • Complete buy → sell ledger per item
  • Items can be sold across multiple sales
This month

Your month, in full

Tap the summary card anywhere and get the whole picture: this month's profit against last month, a 12-month growth chart, every item sold this month, and where your stock stands.

  • Month-over-month growth at a glance
  • Every sale this month, one tap from its details
  • Stock value, YTD and all-time profit
Reports

Reports that answer real questions

Sell-through rate, average profit per item, time to sell, profit by month, year and category, top products — and every chart bar taps through to the exact items behind the number.

  • Overview, yearly and monthly scopes
  • Tap any bar to see its items
  • GST flags recorded for tax time
Add

Add items in seconds

Name, price, a couple of taps for category and platform, photos straight from your library — up to 20 per item, downloaded from iCloud in the background while you keep typing.

  • Up to 20 photos per item, first is the cover
  • Buy fees and GST captured at purchase
  • Record a same-day sale right on the form
