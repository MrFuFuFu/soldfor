SoldFor Privacy Policy

Effective 19 July 2026

SoldFor does not collect any data.

Everything you enter in SoldFor — items, prices, sales, notes and photos — is stored on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, it is synced between your devices through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit private database.

What that means in practice

The developer has no access to your data and cannot read it. It is never sent to, or shared with, any third party. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party software of any kind.

iCloud

Syncing is optional and handled entirely by Apple's iCloud service under your Apple ID. Apple's own privacy policy governs iCloud storage. If you are not signed into iCloud, SoldFor works fully offline and your data never leaves your device.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app removes its data from your device. To remove synced data from iCloud as well, go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and delete SoldFor's data.
